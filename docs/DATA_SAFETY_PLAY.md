## Security Practices

**Is data encrypted in transit?**
N/A - No data transmitted

**Do you provide a way for users to request that their data be deleted?**
N/A - No data collected. Users can clear app data through device settings to remove local preferences.

---

## Play Console Setup

When filling out Data Safety in Google Play Console:

1. "Does your app collect or share user data?" → Select "No"
2. Confirm app doesn't collect any of the listed data types
3. Save declaration

This declaration applies to version 1.0.0 and should be updated if data practices change.
