# Product Steering

## Purpose
Züğürdün Çenesi is a humorous purchasing power calculator that entertains users by showing absurd quantities of everyday items they could buy with any given amount of money.

## Target Audience
- Turkish-speaking mobile users (18-45)
- People who enjoy financial humor and "what if" scenarios
- Lottery dreamers, salary comparers, wealth-curious individuals

## Unique Selling Proposition
- **Original Mascot**: "Çenko" - a witty character that reacts to your money
- **Offline-First**: Works without internet, no tracking, no ads
- **Clean Humor**: Clever without being crude or offensive
- **Rich Picker**: Compare yourself to billionaires for fun

## Core Values
1. **Entertainment First**: This is fun, not financial advice
2. **Privacy Respected**: No data collection, no tracking
3. **Quality Over Quantity**: Premium feel, minimal features done well
4. **Cultural Relevance**: Turkish context, local items and prices

## Humor Boundaries

### ✅ ALLOWED
- Self-deprecating humor about being broke
- Absurdist comparisons (10,000 döners)
- Light sarcasm about prices and inflation
- Witty observations about money
- Playful billionaire comparisons

### ❌ NOT ALLOWED
- Political commentary or satire
- Religious references
- Ethnic/racial jokes
- Offensive or vulgar language
- Mocking specific individuals
- Shaming users for their input amounts

## Success Metrics
- User retention: App opened 3+ times
- Share rate: 10% of calculations shared
- Store rating: 4.5+ stars
- Zero content-related complaints
