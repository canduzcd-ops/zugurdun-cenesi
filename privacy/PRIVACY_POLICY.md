# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Data Collection

**Züğürdün Çenesi** does not collect any personal data.

- ❌ No personal information collected
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No usage analytics
- ❌ No advertising tracking
- ❌ No data sent to third parties

## Local Storage

The app stores only the following on your device:
- Your settings (theme, humor level, exchange rate)
- Edited prices
- Category preferences

This data stays on your device only and is never sent to any server.

## Internet Connection

The app does not require internet connection and sends no data to the internet. It works 100% offline.

## No Account Required

No account creation or login is required to use this app.

For questions: privacy@racalabs.com
